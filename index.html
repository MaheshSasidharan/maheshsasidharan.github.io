Philosophy
KT session
Leadership
At MAQ, I was lucky to have great mentors and POCs and leads and Managers and collegues and..(aur kaun bacha hai!) Many of them helped my better myself in several ways. Those people show traits of Leadership as mentioned here and here.

And I thank you all for being that way.
Respect
One thing that people expect want in life is simply, respect. If you hurt someone by disrespecting that person, then you lack a trait that is fundamental to being human being. Everyone deserves this respect, your juniors, colleagues, friends and seniors. The only people who don’t deserve this respect is the one who lacks this fundamental trait.

Having had witnessed several parties and team outings, I have seen many treat a waiter like he is paid for listening to your harsh words or for that matter when you roll your eyes in disbelief when a security guard asks you to show your ID card (which he is does to ensure Your safety). Why does saving those 5 seconds become something critical for your survival?

Surely there might have been some gaps, or their rules might be questionable, or in worst case, he could have been disrespectful himself. But the locus of control is with you ©. I have seen many people lose control and shout at someone who is of a lower stature than him, yet gladly embracing barb from their boss without battering an eye. So do one thing, try treating everyone, even your subordinates like your boss, give them that fake respect, and then see how your image as a pretentious twat gives way to one with a respectable persona.

A simple case with all of the above scenarios can be seen here. I don’t expect you to be kind through money, just Respect would do. You might not have liked the food\service\ambience\chaddi too tight\fan speed or whatever first world problems you’d have, just don’t put that frustration on someone who is trying to earn his livelihood with such meagre compensation, and simply don’t go there from next time onwards.
Success
There are two kinds of success in this world. Professional and Personal. Professional is just too abstract. Money is not all that matters. There are successful entrepreneur and CEOs I know of who are professionally successful but are aversed by others. Take Donald Trump for instance, rich as rich could be defined, yet invokes a sense of dread and unease when faced with.
(and he is a Republican. So you know they don't think much)
Money is important, but how much is something you have to set because before you know you’d have earned enough just to pass it on to your heir and then you die. :\ People can get rich even by selling tomatoes of a crappy game like Flappy Bird(still don’t know how that got viral). Personal success is when you achieve what you want to, and not what the society defines for you. MKG could have been a rich lawyer, but he became an immortal hero. He succeeded in his life. Bhagat singh succeeded in his life. Malala succeeded in her life. Kailash Satyarthi succeeded in his life. A father/mother who has given decent education to his/her child, has succeeded in life. If that Satisfies you, then that is all you need to do be called a Successful person.

Rich != Successful Life
Satisfaction = Successful Life
Life
Sugar, Spice and Everything Nice. These were the ingredients chosen to create your perfect little sojourn on earth. Don’t forget to live it. Whatever it takes, do everything to make YOURSELF happy.

Smile. :)
Confidence
I have talked with many people here who are pessimistic about your technical or communication skills. Let me emancipate by telling you that YOU WILL be strong enough to feel secure within an year if not few months. Everyone I know of in this company is simply great. Now you might be weak in something, but very soon you can be better than the person who taught you. Everyone has different pace, and the best part, MAQ understands that. So in case your lead or PM calls you and says you have to improve, then just up-shift your gear and take that extra mile, and within next few months, they will call you to solve their issues. I seriously mean EVERYONE of you. So take a chill pill. Because things will get better. Just don’t forget to relish your life in between.

Science
I know being religious gives a sense of hope, but praying everyday to see a shooting star for years and witnessing one after 58 years is not because your prayers were heard, it’s called probability.

Life is much more complicated and appreciative when you try to understand the hows and whys rather than ‘oh choo-mantar, that’s why’. Religion tried to answer complicated things like life and purpose, but with time, science found more believable answers.

I know I sound just like a regular evangelist from a regular faith. But Science is not regular. If A says the Earth is at the center of universe, and B says otherwise, then science accepts only that argument which is more palpable.

So don’t waste this life trying to find a piece of bread which resembles with you religious symbol, rather try to understand how that bread was made - right from electrons and atoms to heating in the over and ready to eat.
